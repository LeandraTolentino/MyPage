My Favourite Books

Introduction to Commutative Algebra

Author: Michael Atiyah

A thoroughly comprehensive book on commutative algebra written by one of the greatest algebraicists. I had a huge amount of fun solving dozens of problems from this book during Christmas holidays.

Economics: The User's Guide

Author: Ha-Joon Chang

My favourite book written by my favourite writer! This book reveals how complicated economics is, revealing all the various issues and solutions posed by schools over the years, such as those by Schumpeter and Keynes.

The Wages of Destruction

Author: Adam Tooze

When I was really young I was deeply fascinated by 20th century conflicts. This book was commonly recommended by historians for those who wish to understand the economy of Germany during WW2, and how its rush for various resources influenced its decisions.
